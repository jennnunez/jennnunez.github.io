You're doing a great job at saving the Earth!

You understand that it is wasteful to use energy when it is not necessary.

However, do you fully understand why?

We use energy when we keep the lights on (even when not needed), burn coal, and use fossil fuels.

Fossil fuels release a gas, called carbon dioxide(CO2), into the air.

CO2 is bad for the environment because, when in the atmosphere, it causes temperatures to rise all over the world.

As it gets hotter, the glaciers in the artic will begin to melt.

This has already started in the artic. If it continues at its current (alarming) speed, by the year 2150, global temperature will be five to eleven degrees warmer.

Many species of animals will not be able to adjust to the new environment; even the human race may become extinct.
