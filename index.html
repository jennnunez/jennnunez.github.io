Hello there! Welcome to my website!

My name is Jennifer Nunez and I am currently an undergraduate student at San Jose State University. I have been given the opportunity to work with a variety of traditional and nontraditional mediums. My work tends to focus on social issues such as: obesity, pollution, crime, isolation/depression, etc. I also enjoy fabricating loose abstracted portraits, landscapes, and still lives on wooden panels with oil paint. However, I am not limited to just painting as I enjoy working with photography, digital media, and other mediums as well; I am also skilled in creating three-dimensional sculptures.
