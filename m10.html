HOLY COW!

Cut back on the beef consumption!

Raising cows, chickens, and other animals for food requires a great deal of land, food, energy, and water.

Production for these animals cause greehouse-gases, that trap and hold heat (i.e. carbon dioxide) to be released and increase rates of global warming.

Eating less meat would persuade companies to raise less livestock, which would in return, cause less gases to be released, lands to be taken from wildlife, and energy to be used.
