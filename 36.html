You're on the right track!

Use your electricity ONLY when necessary. For example, turn your lamp on when it is too dark for you to read your assignments.

Using just enough energy helps prevent the overuse of fossil fuels, which release carbon dioxide gas into the air.

CO2 is bad for the environment because, when in the atmosphere, it causes temperatures to rise all over the world.

It has already started and if it continues at its current alarming speed, by the year 2150, global temperature will be five to eleven degrees warmer.

Once that happens, many species of animals(maybe even humans) may become extinct.
